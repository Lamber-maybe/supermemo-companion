The 3 Key Facts About SuperMemo

[edit | edit source]
  1. Learn faster, remember for years: SuperMemo is a powerful learning method that helps you retain knowledge for life — not just for exams.
  2. Available everywhere: Use SuperMemo on Windows, Android, iOS, or right in your web browser.
  3. Powered by science: SuperMemo is built on the science of long-term memory and decades of research.

How SuperMemo Works

[edit | edit source]
  1. Capture what matters: Turn facts, ideas, or anything you want to remember into learning material with SuperMemo.
  2. Test your memory: SuperMemo regularly checks your recall and builds a detailed model of your memory performance.
  3. Review at the perfect time: Based on your memory strength, SuperMemo schedules reviews just before forgetting sets in.
  4. Save time and effort: With the minimum number of reviews and no wasted effort, you'll be amazed at how much you can learn — and remember.

SuperMemo: More Than Just Repetition

[edit | edit source]

In addition to smart review scheduling, SuperMemo 20 for Windows gives you a full toolkit to power up your brain:

  • read and remember huge amounts of information using incremental learning
  • learn with images, audio, and video
  • track and analyze your sleep
  • optimize learning based on your sleep patterns
  • improve problem-solving with concept maps
  • boost your creativity with neural creativity
  • plan your days for productivity and minimal stress (see: Planning productive days)
